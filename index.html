Data Access FAQ

 

Is my data shared?

No, data is never shared with anyone unless you grant permission. You may grant or revoke permission for this vendor at any time.

What kind of data is Dataium collecting?

Dataium is the single source provider for observed online automotive shopping behavior. This data is used to help automotive professionals learn more about thier customers and prospects.

How can I benefit?

Dataium and their partners offer some of the most advanced optics available anywhere. Visibility into website performance with contexutal benchmarks with others with like-franchises at the DMA and national level.

Cars.com has partnered with Dataium, the largest aggregator of online automotive shopping behavior, to leverage the most comprehensive online automotive data platform available today. Dataium is an objective third-party, with the ability to provide tremendous insight into your website prospects; and they can do this because they collect data from over 20 Million auto shoppers each month.


Through this relationship, Cars.com will be able to provide you with new and improved tools to turn today’s website shoppers into tomorrow’s buyers. To make this happen, Cars.com will require access to the data being collected from your website via Dataium's visitor recognition script, but we can only access this data with your permission. If you authorize Dataium to allow access for Cars.com, please fill out the form below and click the SUBMIT button.

 

Next Steps:

 

If you already have Dataium’s code installed on your website, then Cars.com will now be able to access this data to offer improved products and services. If Dataium’s code is NOT installed on our website, please follow the detailed instructions from Dataium on how to have your website provider install the code. Simply forward the instructions to your website provider. The code must be installed before Dataium can begin collecting and sharing your website data with Cars.com.

 

Thank you very much for your cooperation. We look forward to helping your business grow.
